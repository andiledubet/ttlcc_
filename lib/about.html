Our calling

Established in 2018

The Technology Transfer Licensing and Commercialization Centre is responsible for Technology Transfer in terms of commercialization of inventions and discoveries, Tech-Licensing and business ventures including, but not limited to, joint venture partnerships, venture capital. It is responsible for the management of the Institute’s Research, Development and Innovation outputs and the main role of the TTLCC is to manage the Harare Institute of Technology’s Intellectual Property (HIT IP) & Innovation Policy. This role includes advising on the synthesis and extraction of registrable IP in staff and student projects. TTLCC is the hub for venture mentoring services and establishment of a cross functional technopreneurship forum.

In-Depth

Technology Transfer, Licensing and Commercialisation

Technology Transfer

Technology transfer is the process of disseminating technology from the party that owns or holds it to the TTLCC in an attempt to transform inventions and scientific outcomes into new products and services that benefit society. IP protection gives Harare Institute of Technology capacity to market their inventions, attract funding, seek industrial partners and assure dissemination of new technologies through means such as licensing or creation of start-ups for the benefit of society.

Licensing

Licensing is a business arrangement, wherein on behalf of your company the TTLCC authorizes another company by issuing a license to temporarily access its intellectual property rights, i.e. manufacturing process, brand name, copyright, trademark, patent, technology, trade secret, etc. for adequate consideration and under specified conditions.



Commercialisation

After assessing a project or system and a product or service has been fully developed we introduce the new product or production method into commerce, making it available on the market. We faciltate a move from the laboratory into commerce. Upon the product launch of a new product- advertising, sales promotion, and other marketing efforts are adopted to encourage commercial adoption of the product or method.
