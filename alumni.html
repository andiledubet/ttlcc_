Commercial Alumni Network

The HIT Commercial Alumni Network is a hub for HIT alumni to network, connect and engage with the HIT community (staff & students) and fellow alumni in industry. We seek to establish and cultivate meaningful collaborations to help HIT achieve its mandate of developing, transferring, incubating and commercialising technology for the rapid industrialisation of Zimbabwe. The network exists to promote technopreneurial advancement through workshops, trainings, business opportunities, tech-business events and mentorship. We invite alumni of HIT operating businesses to join us in the institute's aspiration to become a world-leading innovation & technopreneurial university.
