MYCOCULTURE TECHNOLODIES

Smallholder farmers and resource-disadvantaged communities in Zimbabwe can cultivate mushrooms this enables them to have a balanced diet at a relatively inexpensive cost. Edible mushrooms rank above all vegetables and legumes (except soybeans) in protein content and have significant levels of Vitamin B and C, and are low in fat. Mushroom cultivation also enables farmers to utilize organic substrates that would otherwise be regarded as waste products. Mushroom spawn and grow bags enable individuals and small holder farmers to grow their own mushrooms thereby increasing mushroom production. This will in turn promote safe mushroom consumption and year-round income generation.
